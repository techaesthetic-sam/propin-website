How to Delete Your Account

This page is publicly accessible and does not require you to log in.

Delete from the Propin App (Recommended)

  1. Open the Propin app on your device
  2. Go to the Settings tab
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  4. Read the information about what will be deleted
  5. Confirm your decision by tapping "Delete My Account"
  6. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted

Alternative Method: You can also request account deletion by emailing us at frontfootlabs@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Delete Specific Data Without Deleting Your Account

Propin allows users to delete certain data without deleting their account. This can be done directly within the mobile app.

  • Property listings posted by the user can be deleted individually.

Other data such as messages, profile information, and activity history are deleted only when the account is deleted.

Important Information

  • Irreversible Action: Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use Propin again.
  • Immediate Effect: Account deletion takes effect immediately when done through the app.
  • Email Requests: Deletion requests via email are processed within 7 business days after verification.
  • Legal Retention: Some limited information may be retained only as required by applicable laws (such as tax, legal, or regulatory obligations) and will not be used for any other purpose.

Before You Go
